About

Chores At Your Door helps you find Chore-istas - people who do the chores you have been putting off

Chores At your Door.

We all have those nagging tasks we really, really don't want to do today but they have to get done! How cool would it be to get someone to do them for you?

Introducing Chore-istas

They are our crack team of doers who like doing things.

You name chores around the house and they will do them for you. These chores typically take about 1-2 hours to finish and you can ask for a chatty (if you like making new friends) or chatfree (if you prefer quiet) Chore-ista.

Our typical customer asks Chore-istas to do things like

  • tidy up a room
  • laundry+iron
  • water their plants
  • wash their car
  • shopping assistance for girls or guys
  • setting up a party
